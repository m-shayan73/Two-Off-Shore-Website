Thanking Two Offshore Marine's O&G Frontliners

Working on offshore O&G platform is considered one of the most challenging careers. Given the challenges posed by this pandemic, there have been increasing risks in what is already one of the world's most hostile environments.

We are proud of Two Offshore Marine's Operations department and Offshore Crew aka 'Frontliners' who have embraced these challenges as their 'new normal' to deliver their work efficiently, safely and reliably to our customers, during these difficult times.

We would not be able to do this without our 'Frontliners'.

We would like to give a shout out to all of them for their continuous effort, bravery, sacrifices & commitment.

A BIG thank you to the team & the rest of the O&G 'frontliners'.

Introduction Overview

Two Offshore Marine is a 100% Malaysian-owned offshore services and solutions provider of the highest competency and quality in the Malaysian oil and gas industry.

Since its inception in 2016, it has quickly built a stellar reputation within the industry, thanks to its dedicated core of seasoned professionals, an excellent management team, and a modern fleet of vessels that meet or surpass industry standards.

Its dedication towards achieving best practises in Health, Safety and Environment (HSE) has distinguished the company further.
