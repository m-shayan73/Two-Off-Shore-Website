OUR TOP PRIORITY

RAISING THE BAR IN
HSE STANDARDS

Two Offshore Marine’s dedication towards best practises in HSE has helped built and strengthened the company’s reputation.

We promote a culture of being conscientious and responsible corporate citizens, applying a rigorous set of HSE procedures in all aspects of our operations, from our marine crew to our sub-contractors.

We place equal emphasis on green practises within our operational framework, doing our part to make our industry more sustainable.

On top of that, we promote a culture that drives exemplary behaviour in incorporating HSE as part of our daily life and activities. We have a comprehensive set of our daily life and activities. We have a comprehensive set of business principles and rigorous standards covering health, safety, security, the environment and social performance and take great pride in maintaining the highest standards of HSE without compromising quality. We believe in the importance to demonstrate the highest quality in our performance and business delivery that meet or exceed our customer’s needs and expectations.

Best practices are effectively implemented and monitored in adherence to the defining Policies and HSE Manual allowing the management to review and take necessary action to ensure we meet our objective.

Our HSE Activities:

  • Medical talk by Competent Medical Advisor
  • Safety talk by HSE
  • Implementing the practice safe environment at premises
  • Stress management at premises
  • Other interesting activity that promote the HSE in daily life.

Two Offshore Marine are currently members of the Malaysia Shipowners’ Association (MASA), as well as the international Marine Contractors Association (IMCA).

As a part of commitment in improving our overall business operations, we have successfully obtained accreditation of ISO 9001 through a recognised International Body. We continue to benchmark our process with the industry Standards to ensure that the best practices are implemented and executed consistently within the Business.
