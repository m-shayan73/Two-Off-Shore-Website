Our Profile

Please do not hesitate to click on the right button if you want to discuss ways we could help you reach your business objectives.

Two Offshore Marine is a 100% Malaysian-owned offshore services and solutions provider of the highest competency and quality in the Malaysian oil and gas industry. Since its inception in 2016, it has quickly built a stellar reputation within the industry, thanks to its dedicated core of seasoned professionals, an excellent management team, and a modern fleet of vessels that meet or surpass industry standards. Its dedication towards achieving best practises in Health, Safety and Environment (FISE) has distinguished the company further. Offering a wide array of services in support of the oil and gas landscape, Two Offshore Marine s at the forefront of growth poised to sweep the region in the 2020s and beyond.

OUR MISSION

  • To ensure our clients experience the best value from our high standards of service and superior product offerings.
  • To be second to none in Health, Safety & Environment standards among Malaysian OGSE companies.
  • To become an industry icon in good governance, exceptional services and an inspiring place to work.
  • To continually build value for our shareholders and stakeholders by exploring and investing in opportunities in the Malaysian Oil & Gas industry and other synergistic industries.

OUR VISION

To become a globally respected Malaysian brand regarded for exceptional service and reliability in the Oil and Gas service industry.

WHAT WE BELIEVE

  • Customer-Centric

    A commitment to never stop improving our services and our solutions so that we are always the first choice partner to our customers.

  • Forward Thinking

    A relentless and passionate drive to keep moving forward, innovating and growing value for our company and shareholders.

  • United

    A guiding belief that our company and our people are one and the same and that we can only move forward and grow together.

  • Principled

    A shared responsibility to always operate with the highest moral standards and integrity in all our daily interactions, both internal and external.

  • Conscientious

    A solemn obligation to set the bar for Health, Safety and Environment quality in all aspects of our operations, creating a nurturing work environment and doing our part to help create a more sustainable planet.
